Who We Are

Wujiang Runsun Textile is a leading yarn and fabric manufacturer, specializing in high-quality textiles crafted with sustainability and innovation in mind. With years of expertise, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for global clients in fashion, home textiles, and technical fabrics.

Our commitment lies in delivering eco-friendly and durable textile solutions that meet the highest international standards while ensuring responsible production practices that respect nature and communities.
